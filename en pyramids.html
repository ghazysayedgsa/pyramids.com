what is a pyramid?

A pyramid is a polyhedral geometric shape with one of its faces being a polygon called the base of the pyramid. The pyramid results from connecting the corners of a four-sided or three-sided base to a single point called the apex. The most famous shape of the pyramid’s base is the square base shape.
